Department Members

All members of UP AdCore belong to a certain department, selected when they get accepted as members, and renewed every semester. They can serve either as a manager of that department in different teams, a project head, or an AVP.

Department Projects

UP AdCore's projects are classified into departments, depending on the project's objective and contribution to the organization.

Creatives (CRT)

The Creatives department is the design powerhouse of the organization that brings to life ideas of the different departments through the production of quality creative materials. This department also ensures the competence of members by housing all the training initiative projects of the organization.

AdSchool

AdSchool is UP AdCore's official training vessel for effective visual communication. The team shall organize on and off campus learning sessions which will allow our members and future members to develop their design sensibilities and to equip them with competitive creative skills to come up with more innovative and quality works.

AdVance

AdVance brings the advertising world closer to AdCore members by giving them a glimpse of how advertising, marketing, and other corporate agencies work in order to have a better knowledge of advertising as a career path. The team provides learning opportunities about about contemporary, effective, and responsible advertising through seminars and talks conducted by reputable advertising professionals.

Human Resource (HR)

The Human Resources department focuses on three major aspects: (i) Taking care of members, future members, and alumni of the org, (ii) Disseminating information to the organization in an efficient manner, and (iii) Implementing the grading system for both future members and members. That is why the Human Resources Department is also the Heart of the organization as it caters to the lifeblood of the org - its people.

{% include hr.html %}

AdDition

AdDition is the recruitment arm of the organization responsible for pooling in quality future members by spearheading the application process throughout the whole semester. The team is in-charge of grading and guiding the growth of deserving future members, ensuring that they understand and experience the work and play environment of AdCore.

AdHesive

AdHesive accommodates to the growth and welfare of the heart of AdCore—its members. The team initiates activities that cater to the wellbeing of the members and their passion for advertising. They also spearhead team-building activities and events such as The Great AdCore Friday, AdMeet, and the Mem-Future Mem Team Building Session.

AdHere

AdHere is the alumni relations arm of the org which serves as a bridge between the organization’s alumni and its current stakeholders. It maintains constant communication with and involvement of the alumni through initiatives like Made in AdCore, Alumni Mentorship sessions, and Alumnight. Apart from this, the team is also responsible for ensuring a memorable send-off for its graduating members.

AdVocate

AdVocate is AdCore's public service announcement (PSA) arm which has a specific thrust and cause for one semester. This project adheres to the organization's goal of promoting effective and responsible advertising through a partnership with a client and/or an organization for the development of PR events and campaigns. Juiceletter

Juiceletter is UP AdCore's official multimedia outlet for all things advertising, lifestyle, and pop culture. It provides its readers with timely and insightful content about the latest trends, news, and key personalities in the field.

University Job Fair

University Job Fair (UJF) is a yearlong event that aims to provide job and internship opportunities to UP students across all colleges. UJF is organized by the Office of Counseling and Guidance, USC, UP JPIA, and UP Adcore as its sole promotional arm. Packed with insightful talks, lectures, mock interviews, and summits year-round, the UJF Team seeks to provide effective and multi-functional promotional materials and executions that best suit the objectives of the project.

UP Fair

UP Fair is an annual weeklong music festival featuring various OPM artists, both established and up-and-coming ones. The UP Fair Team serves as the official promotional arm of the University Student Council for the whole event. With the vision of highlighting social every night, the UP Fair team seeks to provide well-crafted relevant promotional materials to contribute to the event's success. UP Fair 13.2 is directly catered to the execution of promotions for UP Fair 2019.

APPLICATION PROCESS

Your application process is as simple as spelling ADCORE. Go through with the following steps and be a part of this creativity.

A

This is 15% of your grade. Complete the 6 hours during Tambay Week, as well as a total of 30 hours for the whole semester, and attend UP AdCore events.

D

A buddy family (composed of 2-3 members and 3-6 future members) will have to accomplish buddy family tasks. Each task has corresponding points, and each family will have to meet the required number of points to be announced by Team AdDition each month. This is 10% of your grade.

C

Earn 15% of your grade by asking the members to sign your sigsheet, especially ExeCore, Team AdDition, and the Project Heads. Design it as creatively as you can. Get to know the members more and enjoy!

O

Offer help to the AdCore teams and be an intern! This is your chance to have hands-on experience in handling an AdCore project and in learning how teams work in the organization. This is 25% of your grade.

R

In advertising, this is what the pitching of ideas is called. This incorporates all that you and your fellow future members have learned throughout the application process as you present to Team AdDition. This will earn you 25% of your grade.

E

This is 10% of your grade. This will test your knowledge of UP AdCore and your dedication to the organization.


UP Advertising Core holds its application process every semester. If you are a UP Diliman student looking for an avenue to express your creativity, this might just be the organization for you! Keep posted on our Facebook page to learn more about how to apply to UP AdCore.
